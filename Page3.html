Break fast menu

Jollibee's breakfast menu offers a variety of delicious options with a Filipino twist. From the popular Jollysilog and Longganisa Meal to the Breakfast Sandwich and Pancakes, Jollibee serves up hearty and flavorful breakfast dishes. Whether you're craving crispy fried chicken, juicy beef tapa, or classic bacon and eggs, Jollibee has something to satisfy every morning craving. With a choice of sides like garlic rice and sunny-side-up eggs, accompanied by their famous brewed coffee or fruit juice, Jollibee's breakfast menu provides a tasty and affordable start to your day..

Meals

Jollibee meals offer a variety of tasty and satisfying options. From their iconic Chickenjoy and flavorful Yumburger to the unique Jolly Spaghetti and Palabok Fiesta, Jollibee has something for everyone's cravings. Their Burger Steak provides a hearty and comforting choice, while the Peach Mango Pie serves as a delightful dessert. Whether you're in the mood for fried chicken, burgers, noodles, or a sweet treat, Jollibee's usual meals deliver delicious flavors that capture the essence of Filipino cuisine.

Desserts

Jollibee's dessert menu offers a delectable variety of sweet treats. From the beloved Peach Mango Pie to creamy sundaes like the Chocolate Sundae Twirl and indulgent Halo-Halo Sundae, Jollibee has a dessert for every palate. Their floats, including the popular Chocolate Float, provide a refreshing and fizzy option. Whether you're in the mood for a warm and fruity pastry or a cool and creamy treat, Jollibee's desserts are sure to satisfy your cravings and provide a delightful end to your meal.
